Mapping Crime in College Park

By Erik Swann, Alexandra Pamias, and Clarice Silber

We looked at 6 months worth of data crime in the city of College Park, Md., to determine in which locations most of the crimes occurred. Using the data collected by UMPD and displayed on their website, we created a Google Fusion map so that users can see where the crimes were committed. That map is displayed below
